Our dataset is based on the thirteen users who collected the data during their daily activities. The dataset includes all sensors available in phones and distinguishes five transportation modes: being on a car, on a bus, on a train, standing still and walking.
The dataset was used to build different models, whit different classification algorithms (Decision Tree, Random Forest, Support Vector Machine e Neural Network) reaching a maximum level of accuracy of 96%.

Principal dataset

The following datasets are those used in our tutorial section and in the code that you can find in our Github repository.

Raw Data

The raw data is those collected through the smartphone sensors of our volunteers. These data were not cleaned.
